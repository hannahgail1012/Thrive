Our App

In an effort to make plant care attainable to all users, Thrive utilizes various components for success. Using reminders under the “Care Tracker” program, customers can view what their plants require each day, and can check off each task when completed. The users also have access to detailed care tips about each of their plants under the My Greenhouse screen, allowing them to know their preferences for water, sunlight, and more. If users are wishing to buy new plants, the shop section allows consumers to purchase plants, as well as view informative care information, to allow users to feel that they are getting the best plant for their garden. Consumers can also purchase gardening supplies from the shop section to get a jump start to their plant care journey. Additionally, if users have questions about their plant, or wish to share their gardening experience with others, the Bloom Blog screen offers a social media aspect where consumers can interact with each other within the Thrive app. With these tools used throughout the app, Thrive works to equip users with best-practice strategies to feel confident and encouraged to keep their plant healthy.

The Care Tracker

This section allows you to view tasks coming up to care for your plants. All of your plants in "My Greenhouse" are linked to this section, along with the needs for each plant. Based on the care needs for each of the species in your garden, a task will pop up in the Care Tracker to remind you to feed, water, rotate, and more to keep your plant happy and healthy. After finishing each task, tap the circle to mark that task as complete.

To receive faster notifications without having to open our app, and have immediate plant care reminders, be sure to turn on notifications for Thrive in your phone's settings.






My Greenhouse

In this section, Thrive users can view information about the plants they own, and add new plants to their garden. While the care tracker is great about reminding users what needs are coming up for care within the next couple of days, My Greenhouse shows a detailed description of plant care. This care includes Sunlight preference, water levels, soil type, recommended climate, origin, and more, which is helpful to know to be educated about the plants sharing a home with you.
